Peter Pan

2015

About the production

“I’m Flying”

One of the most recognizable characters in cinema, theatre, and the arts, Peter Pan took to the stage at The American School of Kuwait in 2015 as part of the annual Spring Semester Show.

Sami performed as Toodles, one of the Lost Boys who famously shoots Wendy with an arrow. Filled with adventure, magic, mischievous pirates, and unforgettable characters, the world of Peter Pan and Neverland never disappoints.
