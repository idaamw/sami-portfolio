Biography

Sami graduated from The American School of Kuwait in May 2020, earning his High School Diploma with High Honors and a cumulative GPA of 3.79.

Throughout his academic career, he successfully completed multiple Advanced Placement (AP) courses, including:

  • AP World History
  • AP Chemistry
  • AP Biology
  • AP Human Geography
  • AP Calculus

Beyond the classroom, Sami was deeply involved in the performing arts and public speaking. During his middle and high school years, he appeared in more than eleven theatrical productions, participated in Honors English speeches, and served as Vice President of the Thespian Society. He also represented The American School of Kuwait in NESAC Forensics & Debate, earning multiple awards for his performances in public speaking and competitive debate.

Balancing academic excellence with a passion for theatre, leadership, and communication, Sami's years at The American School of Kuwait laid the foundation for his continued growth as both a performer and a scholar.
